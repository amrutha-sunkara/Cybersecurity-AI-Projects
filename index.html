About This Repository

This repository contains four web-based projects developed as part of the Cybersecurity & Responsible AI module. The projects focus on digital privacy, cybersecurity awareness, AI ethics, fairness evaluation, and responsible AI governance.

Project 15 – Digital Footprint Mapping Generator

Helps users understand their online data exposure, assess privacy risks, and obtain security recommendations.

Project 16 – Phishing Awareness Generator

Creates awareness posters and educational video scripts based on phishing attack scenarios.

Project 17 – AI Bias Evaluation Generator

Examines AI-generated content for bias, fairness concerns, and provides corrective suggestions.

Project 18 – Responsible AI Policy Generator

Generates responsible AI governance policies tailored to organizational requirements.

Key Features

  • Interactive user interfaces
  • Cybersecurity risk assessment
  • AI ethics and fairness analysis
  • Responsible AI governance framework generation
  • Privacy and security recommendations
  • Professional report generation
