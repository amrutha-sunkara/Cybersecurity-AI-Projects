# Cybersecurity & Responsible AI Projects

This repository contains four web-based projects developed under the Cybersecurity & Responsible AI module.

## Live Website
https://amrutha-sunkara.github.io/Cybersecurity-AI-Projects/

## Projects Included

### Project 15 - Digital Footprint Mapping Generator
Analyzes users' digital footprints and evaluates privacy risks.

### Project 16 - Phishing Awareness Poster/Video Script Generator
Generates phishing awareness posters and educational video scripts.

### Project 17 - AI Bias Evaluation Generator
Evaluates AI-generated content for potential bias and fairness concerns.

### Project 18 - Responsible AI Policy Draft Generator
Creates organization-specific responsible AI governance policies.
